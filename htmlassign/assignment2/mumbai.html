Vibrant Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is the financial capital of India and a city that never sleeps. Located on the west coast, it's a bustling, energetic city known for its vibrant film industry, also known as Bollywood.

Key attractions include the Gateway of India, an iconic stone arch built by the British Raj, and the scenic Marine Drive, a C-shaped boulevard along the coast. The city is also famous for its delicious street food, especially Vada Pav and Pav Bhaji, which can be found at every corner.

From the historic Elephanta Caves on an island offshore to the bustling markets of Colaba Causeway, Mumbai offers a diverse range of experiences. Its colonial architecture, vibrant arts scene, and fast-paced lifestyle make it a truly unique destination.
